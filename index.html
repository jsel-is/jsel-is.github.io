Research profile

PhD work at UPC on cloud–edge systems, learning-based control, and reliability in cloud-native environments.

01

About

I am a PhD candidate at the Universitat Politècnica de Catalunya (UPC) working on applied research in cloud–edge distributed systems, with a focus on learning-driven scheduling, autoscaling and reliability under highly volatile workloads. My research bridges large-scale infrastructure with learning-based control and robust optimization, aiming to improve service availability, trustworthiness and performance under uncertainty in cloud-native environments.
